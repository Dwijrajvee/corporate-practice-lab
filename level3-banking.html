Level 3: The "Nuclear" Clauses

The Bank has sent a draft Loan Agreement. They want to be able to recall the loan for any reason. You need to narrow the scope.

⚠️ The Concept: "Events of Default" (EoD)

An EoD allows the Bank to say: "Give me my money back right now."
Your Strategy: Add "Materiality" (only big problems count) and "Thresholds" (only debts over $1M count).

⚔️ Negotiation 1: The "Catch-All" Clause

The Bank wants to call a default if they "feel" insecure. This is too subjective.

"EoD 12.1: An Event of Default occurs if, in the sole opinion of the Bank, there is a Material Adverse Effect on the Borrower's business."
⚔️ Negotiation 2: Cross Default

The Bank says: "If you default on ANY other loan, you default on this one too."

"EoD 13.4: An Event of Default occurs if the Borrower defaults on any indebtedness owed to any third party."
