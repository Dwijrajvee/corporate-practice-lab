Level 1: The Anatomy of a Deal

Before you enter the Data Room, you need to know the documents that structure every M&A transaction.

1. Term Sheet (LOI)

The "Handshake." A non-binding summary of the price and key terms. It allows the buyer to start Due Diligence.

2. Due Diligence (DD)

The "Investigation." Lawyers verify if the company is legally clean (Are taxes paid? Are there lawsuits?).

3. SPA (Share Purchase Agreement)

The "Sale Contract." This transfers ownership from Seller to Buyer. It contains Reps & Warranties (Promises).

4. SHA (Shareholders Agreement)

The "Marriage Contract." If there are multiple owners (Investors + Founders), this decides who controls the Board and who can sell shares.
