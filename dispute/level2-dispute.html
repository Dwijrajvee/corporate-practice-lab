Level 2: The Trigger Notice

A "Section 21 Notice" stops the limitation clock. Review your junior's draft. Fix the errors or the case is dead on arrival.

The Essentials

A Notice MUST have:

1. Clear Demand: State exactly what is owed ($). Vague complaints don't count.
2. Invocation: Explicitly mention "Arbitration Clause X". Don't just say "Legal Action".
3. Nomination: You MUST suggest a name for the Arbitrator (or ask them to suggest one).
Part 1: The Threat
"We are very unhappy with your service. Unless you fix this immediately, we will take appropriate legal action against you in the courts."
Part 2: The Clause
"Since we cannot resolve this dispute amicably, we hereby invoke Clause 14 (Arbitration) of the Agreement dated 01/01/2023."
Part 3: The Arbitrator
"We look forward to hearing from you soon regarding the appointment."
*(No name suggested)*
