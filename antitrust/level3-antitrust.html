Level 3: The Premature Merger

Two Airlines (AirBlue and SkyRed) have signed a merger deal. But the CCI approval is still 6 months away. The CEOs are impatient.

⚠️ The Concept: "Standstill Obligation"

Until the CCI says "Approved," the two companies must compete as strangers.
Rule: You cannot act as one entity yet. This is called "Gun Jumping."

Question 1: IT Integration
"Lawyer, it will take months to merge our IT systems. Can we start migrating SkyRed's customer database into AirBlue's servers today so we are ready for Day 1?"
Question 2: Joint Marketing
"Can we launch a joint marketing campaign saying 'AirBlue and SkyRed are now One Big Family'?"
Question 3: Deal Planning
"Okay, fine. Can we at least set up a 'Clean Team' of consultants to *plan* the integration strategy on paper, without actually moving data?"
