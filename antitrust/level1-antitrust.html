Level 1: The Three Sins

Competition Law isn't about protecting competitors. It's about protecting the process of competition. Learn the 3 big rules.

1. Anti-Competitive Agreements (Cartels)

"The Conspiracy." Competitors secretly agreeing to fix prices or limit supply. This is the worst crime.
Penalty: Up to 3x Profit.

2. Abuse of Dominance

"The Bully." Being big (Monopoly) is NOT illegal. But using that size to crush rivals (e.g., Predatory Pricing) IS illegal.

3. Merger Control (Combinations)

"The Checkpoint." If two big companies merge, they must ask the CCI (Competition Commission) for permission before closing the deal.

4. Gun Jumping

"The Premature Celebration." If you start acting like one company (sharing prices/strategy) before CCI approval, you get fined heavily.
