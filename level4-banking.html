Level 4: The Financial Health Check

Before you draft the contract, you must check the math. If the numbers don't add up, the Bank walks away.

1. LTV (Loan to Value)

How "safe" is the loan? If we lend $80 on a $100 house, the LTV is 80%. Banks hate anything above 75%.

LTV = Loan Amount √∑ Asset Value
2. DSCR (Debt Service Coverage)

Can they afford the EMI? If they earn $120 and pay $100 debt, DSCR is 1.2x. Banks demand at least 1.25x.

DSCR = Income (NOI) √∑ Annual Debt Payment
