Level 1: The Language of Debt

Banking lawyers live and die by the "CP Checklist." Learn what it means before you start.

CP (Condition Precedent)

"You get the money IF..."
A list of things the Borrower MUST do before the Bank releases the loan (e.g., provide Board Resolution, Valuation Report).

Security Trustee

In a "Syndicated Loan" (many banks lending together), this entity holds the mortgage/security on behalf of all banks.

Event of Default (EoD)

The "Nuclear Button." If the Borrower does this (e.g., misses payment, goes bankrupt), the Bank can demand ALL money back immediately.

Term Sheet

The "Trailer" before the Movie. A 5-page summary of the deal signed before the 100-page Loan Agreement is drafted.
