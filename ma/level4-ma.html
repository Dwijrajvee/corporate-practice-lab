Level 4: The Cap Table

The Founder says: "I'm raising $2 Million at an $8 Million valuation."
Does the investor get 25% or 20%? It depends on one word.

The Formula

Always calculate the "Post-Money" Valuation first.

Post-Money = Pre-Money + Investment Investor % = Investment √∑ Post-Money
Example

Pre-Money: $8M
Invest: $2M
Post-Money: $10M ($8M + $2M)
Investor gets: $2M / $10M = 20%
