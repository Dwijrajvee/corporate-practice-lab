Level 3: The Shareholder Fight

You represent a Minority Investor (10%). The Founder (60%) wants total control. Protect your exit rights.

⚠️ The Concepts: Tag vs. Drag
  • Tag Along (Good for you): If Founder sells to Google, you can "Tag Along" and sell your 10% too. (Prevents you from being stuck with a stranger).
  • Drag Along (Dangerous): If Founder sells to Google, they can "Drag" you and force you to sell even if you don't want to.
⚔️ Negotiation 1: The "Forced Sale" Trap

The Founder wants the right to force you to sell at ANY price.

"Clause 12 (Drag Along): If the Majority Shareholder decides to sell the Company to a Third Party, the Minority Shareholders MUST sell their shares at the same price offered to the Majority."
⚔️ Negotiation 2: The "Left Behind" Risk

The SHA is missing a clause. The Founder sells 60% to a PE Fund. You are now stuck with a PE Fund you don't know.
