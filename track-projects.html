1. The 3 Acronyms You Will Hear Daily

COD (Commercial Operation Date)

The "Birthday" of the project. Before COD, you are just spending money (construction). After COD, you start making money (selling power).
Why it matters: If you miss the COD deadline, you pay huge penalties.

Tariff (The Price)

2. The "Must-Know" Sections (Electricity Act, 2003)

You don't need to read the whole 200-page Act. Just mark these 3 sections:

  • Section 63 (Bidding): The "Holy Grail." It says if the price was discovered via a transparent auction, the Government must accept it.
  • Section 79 (CERC): This tells you where to file a case. If your plant sells power to multiple states, you go to the Central Commission (CERC), not the State one.
  • Section 14 (Licensing): Who is allowed to transmit power? (Usually needs a license).
